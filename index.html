This is my first experience photographing fireworks. Fireworks held in New York city, but the views are really good from across the river in Jersey City. I went there early to secure a nice spot and I setup my gears. I photographed handfull of good ones and this one is my fav

Beautiful Block Island

A memorable trip to block island with my wife, then girlfriend, during 2012 summer. I really enjoyed photographing this location as you get plenty of subjects to do so. This pic is my favorite among all.

The Mesmerizing Moon

I am big stargazer and moon always mesmerizes me. Since picturing moon is quite a challenge I wanted to give it a try. And I was really happy the way it came out, 3D looking beautiful moon. Many people thinks full moon is the best time to take a picture but I say otherwise. As 3/4th the moon has interesting contrast of light and shadow giving you that 3D effect.
